Subject: July EVEN Year Badge Renewal

﻿

We noticed your badge is expiring in the month of JULY.  According to our records, we show that you were born in an EVEN YEAR, which means we need to resubmit your information for an updated fingerprint case number dated for 2020 within 45 days of your badge expiration.    We need you to complete the following forms attached for your badge renewal:

DISCLAIMER: Some badges have expiration dates that are different from the birthday. Please pay attention to your badge’s expiration date to make sure your forms are submitted on time for your renewal!

 

 

Step 1: 

 

EMAIL the Customs Application Fillable form to UA-IADBadging@united.com

  • DO NOT PRINT THE FORM - We must be able to go into the file to edit it, and we cannot do that if it is printed. It cannot be handwritten and/or scanned. The attached instruction sheet will walk you through filling out the form correctly.

 

  • Email copies of 2 forms of Government issued IDs – (Social Security is no longer considered a form of Gov’t issued ID)  *Please ensure names on the IDs are an exact match*

 

 

Step 2: 

 

In order to minimize person-to-person spread of COVID-19, please send the following forms VIA EMAIL to UA-IADBadging@United.com. If you are unable to send these forms via email, please place the forms in the metal drop box located on the Badging Office back door.

 

  •   CHRC Forms – 3 Pages (form can be handwritten)

·         MWAA Application Form -  Section 1 only (Applicant Information) (form can be handwritten)

·         MWAA questionnaire page

 

To comply with social distancing - If you have questions, please call us before coming into the office.

 

 

Step 3:

 

  • Once the United Badging Office has processed and updated your paperwork; the UA BADGING OFFICE will call or email you that your paperwork is ready for pick-up.
  • You will need to pick up your completed forms from the United Badging Office and BRING 2 FORMS OF GOV’T ID before proceeding to MWAA Pass & ID. 

 

 

 Step 4:

 

  • After you renew your MWAA badge, please return to the United Badging Office to have your new badge photocopied for our Customs Audit.

 

 

It is every employee’s responsibility to fill out their own forms and make sure their badge does not expire.

 

If an employee’s badge expires, the employee is responsible for all fees and loss of pay that may be implemented.

 

Please note badge applications cannot be submitted to MWAA Pass & ID more than 30 days in advance.
